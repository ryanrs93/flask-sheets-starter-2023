About Us

Who Are We?

01

Local

Uncommon Grounds (UG) has been a part of the Georgetown community since 1995, located right across from the University bookstore in the Leavey Center. We're proud to be a local coffee shop, providing a cozy and intimate atmosphere for students and faculty alike.

02

Student Run

We are Students Serving Students. As part of "The Corp", a nonprofit charitable organization, we are part of eight on-campus employers employing over 350 undergraduate students and funding tens of thousands in philanthropic work

03

Use Quality Ingredients

Our commitment to quality extends from our selection of coffees and teas to our assortment of bagels, muffins, croissants, and other pastries. We ensure that our ingredients meet high standards, providing you with the perfect pick-me-up for early mornings and late nights.

04

Creative Hub

UG is not only a place for students to enjoy their coffee but also an opportunity for them to showcase their talents. We host live performances, poetry readings, open-mic nights, and display student artwork, making UG a vibrant hub of creativity on campus.
